Why Favorous?

The meaning of favor means something done or granted out of goodwill; a kind act, friendly or well-disposed regard; it also means a token of love given out in a party. Favorous is everything about all the above. We want to ensure that our brides and grooms have worry-free experience to plan their special day with the help of friendliest team, people they can trust to run the important moments for them!

Faye founded Favorous in 2010 after helping in numerous weddings and events. She realised one of the main factor of successful event is the trust from the clients. Backed with a solid teamwork of the planner and reliable local vendors, her friendly approach played as a big role in the whole setting.

She is always in search of the latest trends, digging new ideas, and never stop listen to the clients wish and request!

Having favorous is like having your best friend organising your big day! XO
